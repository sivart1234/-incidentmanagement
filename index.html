The .ai Opportunity

A domain extension unlike any other

The .ai extension was originally the country-code domain for Anguilla, a British Overseas Territory in the Caribbean. In 2021, Google reclassified .ai as a generic top-level domain — granting it the same search authority as .com worldwide.

Since then, .ai has become the fastest-growing domain extension in the world. Registrations have surged from 60,000 in 2022 to over 632,000 in 2025 — with a renewal rate exceeding 90%, the highest of any TLD. Leading technology companies, AI startups, and Fortune 500 organisations now treat premium .ai domains as permanent strategic assets.

The aftermarket reflects this conviction. The highest recorded .ai domain sale now exceeds $1.5 million, and category-defining names continue to set new benchmarks as AI adoption accelerates across every industry.
